

# AuraSpaces Interior Studio – Luxury Demo Website

A premium, portfolio-focused website for an Indian interior design studio, built as a demo showcase by BrainyWorks. The design will feel high-end, elegant, and metro-level — not template-like.

---

## Design System
- **Colors**: White (#FFFFFF), Warm Beige (#F5F0EB), Charcoal Grey (#2C2C2C), Soft Gold (#C9A96E)
- **Typography**: Elegant serif headings (Playfair Display) + clean sans-serif body (Inter/DM Sans)
- **Feel**: Minimal, airy, luxury — generous whitespace, large imagery, subtle gold accents

---

## Page Sections

### 1. Sticky Demo Banner
- Top bar: "⚡ Demo Website Created by BrainyWorks – We Build Premium Interior Studio Websites"
- CTA button: "Get This Website For My Studio"
- Stays visible on scroll

### 2. Hero Section
- Full-width luxury interior background image (placeholder)
- Large serif headline: "Designing Spaces That Inspire"
- Subtext with gold accent line
- Three CTA buttons: View Portfolio, Get Free Consultation, WhatsApp Us
- Subtle fade-in animations on load

### 3. About Studio
- Split layout: image + text
- Stats row: 12+ Years, 300+ Projects, Residential & Commercial, Custom Design
- Artistic mission quote with elegant styling
- Fade-in on scroll

### 4. Portfolio Grid
- Filterable category tabs: Living Rooms, Bedrooms, Modular Kitchens, Office Interiors, Luxury Villas
- Image grid with hover overlay effect + "View Project" button
- Placeholder images with elegant aspect ratios

### 5. Featured Projects
- 3 large project cards with:
  - Hero image, project name, location, description, cost range
- Alternating left/right layout for visual interest

### 6. Our Process
- 5-step horizontal timeline/stepper:
  - Consultation → Concept Design → 3D Visualization → Execution → Final Handover
- Minimal icons, clean lines, gold accent numbers

### 7. Why Choose Us
- 5 feature cards in grid:
  - 3D Visualization, Premium Materials, On-Time Delivery, Transparent Pricing, Dedicated Manager
- Icons + short descriptions, subtle hover effects

### 8. Client Testimonials
- 4 testimonial cards with:
  - Client name, city, feedback, star rating
- Elegant card design with quote marks and gold accents

### 9. Book Consultation Form
- Elegant lead capture form:
  - Full Name, Phone, Email, Property Type (dropdown), Budget Range (dropdown), Message
- On submit: success toast — "Our Design Consultant Will Contact You Soon. (Demo Only)"
- Gold-accented submit button

### 10. Location & Contact
- Two-column: contact details + Google Maps placeholder
- Studio address, phone, email, business hours
- Strong CTA: "Schedule Your Free Design Consultation"

### 11. Footer
- Multi-column: Quick Links, Services, Portfolio Categories, Contact, Social Icons
- BrainyWorks demo disclaimer prominently displayed
- Dark charcoal background with gold accents
